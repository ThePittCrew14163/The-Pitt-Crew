Outreach

The Pitt Crew has told many people about FIRST. We went to a local school and showed our robot
to the kids who were inspired to participate in FIRST. We demonstrated our robot at a fair where
500+ people possily saw our robot. We held a yard sale where we raised money for our team and
told people about FIRST. And those are just a few of the places we've told people about FIRST!

Fairs

We have gone to many fairs. At each one we've gone to we set up a booth displaying our robot, information about FIRST etc... We were able to answer people's questions about our team and FTC. Overall we told many people about how and why to participate in FIRST.

Videos 

The Pitt Crew has also made videos about FIRST. We won first place for making the best video responding to a FIRST related promt at a tournament we attended our first year! We made a similar video the next year and look to make videos helping other teams with common problems we've encountered.

Schools

The Pitt Crew even went to a school to display our robot. The elemantary school students were very fascinated by the robot. We learned later that the kids actually wanted to start a robotics team with FIST becouse of their experience. The robot inspired them to persue robotics

Co-ops

Our team has earned many awards from the tournaments we've atteneded. We've won an award for entering the best paper explaining our code, an award for making the best video responding to a prompt related to FIRST or FTC, and last but not least we won first place at a tournament our second year!
